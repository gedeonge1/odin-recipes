Vegetable and Fruit Juice

Ingredients

  • 3 large carrots
  • 8 large strawberries
  • 1 large tomato
  • 1 large red bell pepper, stemmed and seeded
  • 1 medium apple
  • 1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger

Instructions

step 1

Place a collection container under the exit spout of a juicer; turn the juicer on. Place carrots into the feeding chute; insert the food pusher and gently guide carrots down the chute. Repeat with strawberries, tomato, bell pepper, apple, lime, and ginger. Turn off the juicer.

step 2

Stir juice in the collection container and serve.
